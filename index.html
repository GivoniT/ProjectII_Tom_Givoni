How Does Financial Status Affect VCE Scores? (2024)

For each region in Victoria, we view the average ICSEA scores—an indicator of socio-educational advantage. Dots represent schools, coloured by their median VCE study score.

What is ICSEA?

ICSEA (Index of Community Socio-Educational Advantage) summarises the relative socio-educational status of a school’s student population. Higher values indicate greater advantage.

What can we learn?

Higher-performing schools cluster around metropolitan Melbourne, where ICSEA scores tend to be higher. Yet the relationship is not absolute: regions with strong socio-educational advantage still contain schools with only moderate median VCE results.
